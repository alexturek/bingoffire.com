FAQ

What's a jianbing and how do you pronounce it?

"Gee-n-bing" Jianbing... are just jianbing. They're just these tasty Chinese pancake/crepe/burritos you get from street vendors in China. We call them bing for short.

Where do you set up?

Around Seattle. Please check out Twitter (@bingoffire) or Weibo for the latest news.

Why'd you get into this?

Because I enjoy eating it myself. I'm not a special snowflake, so surely other people enjoy jianbing too.

Nobody in Seattle was really making it. I've seen it on some menus in restaurants, but it's not the same as getting it from a cart.

Are you Chinese?

Nope. I just enjoy Chinese food. I did spend some time in China, teaching English, traveling, and eating. Lots of eating.

And the name?

We were listening to Johnny Cash, trying to think up names for the cart.
