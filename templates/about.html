What's Bing of Fire?

Thanks Johnny!

What's a jianbing and how do you pronounce it?

"Gee-n-bing" Jianbing... are these tasty Chinese pancake/crepe/burrito thing you get from street vendors in China early in the morning.

Where do you set up?

Around Seattle, though I'm in different locations most days. I'd recommend hitting up the Twitters (@bingoffire) or Facebook for the latest news.

Why'd you get into this?

Selfish reasons mostly. I was walking through a US airport early in the morning saying, "Man I would love a jianbing right now." Nobody in Seattle was really making it. I've seen it on some menus in restaurants, but it's not the same as getting it from a cart.

Are you Chinese?

Nope. I just enjoy Chinese food. I did spend some time in China, teaching English, traveling and eating. Lots of eating.

Future plans?

Night market! I love the idea of a bunch of food trucks and carts in the same parking lot creating a night market where friends/family can go and pick out their own food. There'd be lights, tables, some canopies. A friendly atmosphere for all. Love it.
